## Core Requirements for Infinite Sustainability
To establish an infinitely repeatable loop of human civilization, global activity must adapt to three key operational metrics:
## 1. Net-Zero Carrying Capacity [12] 
Humanity's [Ecological Footprint](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sustainability_metrics_and_indices) must drop to match Earth's exact biocapacity. We currently consume resources at a rate equivalent to 1.7 Earths per year, meaning we are actively draining finite ecological capital rather than living on the renewable "interest." [2, 13, 14, 15, 16] 
## 2. Absolute Circular Resource Loops
Infinite sustainability requires a complete halt to linear waste generation. Every synthetic or natural item manufactured must fit a strict Life Cycle Assessment (LCA), ensuring 100% upcycling or non-toxic environmental degradation. [1, 17, 18] 
## 3. Energy Equilibrium
All human power grids must function strictly using ambient energy harvesting (solar, wind, tidal, geothermal, and advanced localized microgrids) without altering the planet's core radiative forcing balance. [10, 19]
